Welcome To The Class of 2020 Graphic Design Grad Show

ABOUT

Every year, graduating graphic design seniors at Central Washington University showcase their portfolios. Because of Covid-19 and social distancing, it was not possible to have it in person this year. So instead we are having this digitial show!
