Welcome To The Class of 2020 Graphic Design Grad Show

ABOUT

Every year, graduating graphic design seniors at Central Washington University showcase their portfolios. Because of the Covid-19 and social distancing it was not possible to do it the usual way. So instead, here is our show, but virtual.
