ABOUT

Every year, graduating graphic design seniors at Central Washington University showcase their portfolios. Because of Covid-19 and social distancing, it was not possible to have it in person this year. So instead we are having this digital show!

Matt Conrardy

Artist/ Designer

Matt Conrardy is a multidisciplinary artist and designer with a current focus on illustrated music and event posters, apparel graphics, branding, and multi-media painting. Striving to split his creative energy between both commercial and non-commercial pursuits, his approach emerged out of the intentional desire to blend classical training and digital techniques. Combining expressive hand-drawn illustration and typography with concept-driven design and often layered storytelling, his approach and visual style is unique, effective, and refreshingly human.
